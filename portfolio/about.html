About JobSpy

JobSpy is a job scraping library with the goal of aggregating all the jobs from popular job boards with one tool.

It scrapes job postings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, Google, ZipRecruiter, & other job boards concurrently and aggregates them in a dataframe.

Supports proxies to bypass blocking.
